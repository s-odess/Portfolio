Perspective

Scaling systems and effectively auditing them require different approaches. Production environments often hide certain failure modes from internal evaluation mechanisms.

Human oversight from outside the primary optimization loop surfaces these gaps. This function remains essential even as models improve — it simply evolves in form and focus.
